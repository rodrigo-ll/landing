About LeanLog

We've run logistics, scaled distribution, and shipped AI into regulated environments.

LeanLog is built by a small team of operators and AI engineers. We've scaled ecommerce operations from zero, deployed optimization and machine learning inside regulated industries, and built AI agents for the Department of Defense.

Across every operations-heavy business we've worked with or talked to, the same pattern shows up: the system of record holds the records, but the actual work happens between the systems. Spreadsheets. Email threads. Knowledge that lives in a few people's heads. Migrating to a new ERP doesn't fix it. It moves the pain.

So we're building the layer that captures those decisions, executes them, and gets sharper over time. Models swap underneath. Systems migrate. The work keeps running.
