02 — The problem

3PLs and Distributors Are Squeezed From Every Direction.

Rising salaries, greater rent, and intense competition have squeezed margins to near zero. The software that's supposed to help costs more to implement than years of licensing.

01 / 03

Margins under siege

You compete on price and service. But every carrier dispute, short-pay, and manual PO erodes the margin you fought to win. There's no room for back-office inefficiency.

02 / 03

Labor is your biggest cost

For every shipment, there are 5–10 back-office touches: scheduling calls, supplier follow-ups, invoice reconciliation. That's a full-time employee's work, repeated daily across every client or vendor.

03 / 03

Your WMS doesn't cover the half of it

A WMS tracks inventory. It doesn't call the carrier when a delivery is late, dispute the invoice, or follow up with a vendor on a short shipment. That still falls on your team.

03 — The solution

Runs your back office the way a great ops hire would.

No new software to learn. No process changes. LeanLog slots into how your team already operates, through voice and text, from day one.

Works by talking

Your dispatcher, ops manager, or buyer just sends a message — "schedule Dock 3 for Tuesday," "dispute the Acme invoice," "reorder SKU 4821." The agent handles the rest: calls, emails, records, follow-ups.

Full visibility

Every carrier call, vendor negotiation, PO, and invoice is logged automatically. Your team and your clients always know where things stand — no chasing updates.

You stay in control

You set the rules. High-value POs require approval; routine reorders execute automatically. Works for 3PLs managing client SLAs and distributors managing vendor terms alike.
